About Bihar Polytechnic

Bihar Polytechnic (DCECE) ek state level entrance exam hai jo Bihar ke students ke liye hota hai. Is exam ke through aap diploma engineering, paramedical aur anya courses me admission le sakte hain. Ye exam Bihar Combined Entrance Competitive Examination Board (BCECEB) dwara conduct kiya jata hai.

Important Details

Exam Name DCECE Bihar Polytechnic 2026
Board BCECEB
Course Diploma Engineering / Paramedical
Location Bihar
